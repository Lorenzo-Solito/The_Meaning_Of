Who we are

Welcome everyone!
We are Elisa and Greta, two sisters and two women who have combined their knowledge and skills to create The Meaning Of, a project born in 2020, just when the Covid-19 pandemic forced us to take some time for ourselves at home. Elisa is psychologist, psychotherapist in training and an expert in adolescents and young adults. She is a lover of knowledge, arts, fashion and beauty. Greta has a degree in Linguistic and Cultural Mediation and at the moment she is attending a master in Digital Communication. She is determined and a lover of life, she never stops at the first obstacle.

Our mission

We have a mission: to go further.
Let's look for the meaning of events, moments and personalities. We believe that knowledge and curiosity are the most powerful tools of our generation.
Let's start from here, let's start wondering why.
