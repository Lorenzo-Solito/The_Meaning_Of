What you should do if you are a close contact of a Covid-19 case and if you have no symptoms (in Italy):
If you have three doses of covid vaccine or two doses for less than four months: no quarantine but you have to wear the FFP2 mask (self-monitoring). To exit self-monitoring, you need a negative swab after at least five days or on the fourteenth day without a swab.
If you have two doses of covid vaccine for more than four months: five days of quaratine plus a negative swab (or fourteen days of quarantine without a swab).
If you are not vaccinated: ten days of quarantine plus a negative swab (or fourteen days of quarantine without a swab). If you have some doubts, contact us in the email below or go on our Instagram page (click on the symbol below).
